About

Hi! Thanks for visiting! I am Phuong. I am an avid Converse Chuck Taylor All Star collector. Converse Chuck Taylor All Stars are also known simply as "chucks". I have been collecting chucks since high school.

Surprisingly, it was not love at first sight with chucks for me. I remember my parents taking me to Journeys to buy new shoes for the start of the school year. My sisters all owned chucks already and I hated them before I grew to love them. They just looked like clown shoes! It wasn't until I came across the on-sale pair of chocolate plaid chucks at that Journeys store did it begin to pique my interest.

I received my second pair of chucks as a high school graduation gift from my parents. I loved them because they were my first pair of high tops and they were red (my favorite color). Throughout college, Converse continued releasing more collaborations and by my sophmore year in college I was at over 30 pairs. I worked part-time during college and the Converse collecting had plagued me before I even realized it. I simply loved the different colors and designs they had. I fell more in love with the patterned chucks as they were like art to me. As college came and went, I continued to receive chucks as gifts from family during the holidays and my birthday. Today, I currently own a little over 150 pairs of chucks. I don't have plans to purchase anymore unless it's a collaboration or design I really love.

These chucks are my passion and one of my dream jobs is to work at Converse one day. I may consider it when I retire from my full-time job in a few decades!
